Understanding Block vs Inline Elements

This page demonstrates the difference between block-level elements and inline elements in HTML. Block-level elements, like <p> and <h1>, take up the full width of their container and start on a new line. Inline elements, such as <a>, <i>, and <b>, only take up as much width as their content and do not force new lines.
